# 🏦 Bank Marketing Campaign Analysis – Power BI

Interactive **Power BI dashboard** analysing customer subscription behaviour, campaign effectiveness, and financial insights using the **UCI Bank Marketing Dataset**.

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyse bank marketing campaign performance.
* Understand customer subscription behaviour.
* Identify high-performing customer segments.
* Analyse subscription rate by **call duration** and **campaign contacts**.
* Generate actionable business insights using Power BI.

## 📊 Dataset

* **Dataset:** Bank Marketing
* **Source:** UCI Machine Learning Repository
* **Records:** 45,211
* **Features:** 16
* **Target:** Term Deposit Subscription (`y`)

🔗 [UCI Bank Marketing Dataset](https://archive.ics.uci.edu/dataset/222/bank+marketing)

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* **Power BI**
* **Power Query**
* **DAX**
* **Microsoft Excel**

## 🧹 Data Preparation

* Cleaned and transformed raw data using Power Query.
* Standardized data types and categorical values.
* Created **Call Duration Categories**.
* Developed DAX measures for KPIs and subscription analysis.

## 🔑 Key Insights

* **45.21K** customers analysed with **5.29K subscribers**.
* Overall subscription rate: **11.7%**.
* **18–25 age group** recorded the highest subscription rate at **23.95%**.
* **Single customers** had the highest marital-status subscription rate at **14.95%**.
* **Tertiary-educated customers** achieved a **15.10%** subscription rate.
* **10+ minute calls** achieved approximately **48%** subscription rate.
* Subscription rate declined from **14.60%** with 1 contact to **5.81%** with 6+ contacts.
* Customers with a successful previous campaign outcome recorded **over 60%** subscription rate.
* Subscribers had an average balance of **₹1.804K**, compared with **₹1.304K** for non-subscribers.

## 💡 Business Impact

The analysis highlights the importance of **customer targeting, effective call engagement, previous campaign outcomes, and optimized contact frequency** for improving marketing campaign performance.
